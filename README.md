# VIDYA - Educational Guidance & Discovery Platform

Welcome to VIDYA, a web-based platform built to help students find the right college and course without the hassle of searching through multiple sources.

## About

Finding accurate information about colleges and courses is genuinely difficult, especially for students in smaller towns and rural areas. VIDYA was built to solve that problem.

It brings everything together in one place — search and filter colleges by course, location, and other criteria, with information that stays up to date. Instead of spending hours across different websites, students can find what they need quickly and make better decisions about their education.

## What it does

- Search and filter colleges and courses based on your preferences
- Get real-time, up-to-date academic information
- Designed to be simple and accessible for students everywhere

## Built with

- HTML, CSS, JavaScript
- Firebase Firestore
- Deployed on Vercel and GitHub Pages

## Background

 The idea came from a real problem — students around me were struggling to find reliable information about colleges and had to rely on word of mouth or outdated websites. VIDYA is my attempt to fix that.
